OUR PERSONAL INJURY PRACTICE

Holding Negligent Parties Accountable

When someone else's negligence causes you serious injury, the consequences can be devastating — mounting medical bills, lost wages, chronic pain, and emotional suffering. At RV Litigation Group PC, we believe that injury victims deserve aggressive legal representation to recover the full compensation they are owed. We fight insurance companies and at-fault parties so you can focus on healing.

Our personal injury attorneys serve clients throughout the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Jose, Santa Clara County, Gilroy, Morgan Hill, San Francisco, and the greater Silicon Valley region. From car accidents on Highway 101 to workplace injuries in construction zones, we handle the full spectrum of personal injury cases with the same tenacity and preparation we bring to every area of our practice.

We work on a contingency fee basis for personal injury cases — you pay nothing unless we recover compensation for you. From your initial consultation through settlement negotiations or trial, our team is committed to maximizing your recovery while keeping you informed at every step.

PRACTICE AREAS

Personal Injury Services

Car Accidents

Rear-end collisions, intersection crashes, highway pileups, and hit-and-run accidents. We fight insurance companies to get you full compensation.

Motorcycle Accidents

Motorcycle riders face catastrophic injuries from negligent drivers. We pursue maximum damages for road rash, fractures, and traumatic brain injuries.

Truck Accidents

Commercial truck and big rig collisions cause devastating injuries. We hold trucking companies and drivers accountable under state and federal regulations.

Slip & Fall / Premises Liability

Property owners must maintain safe conditions. We pursue claims for falls caused by wet floors, broken stairs, poor lighting, and hazardous conditions.

Wrongful Death

When negligence takes a loved one, families deserve justice. We pursue wrongful death claims for funeral costs, lost income, and loss of companionship.

Dog Bites

California's strict liability dog bite law holds owners responsible regardless of the animal's history. We recover damages for bites, maulings, and attacks.

Pedestrian Accidents

Pedestrians struck by vehicles suffer life-altering injuries. We hold negligent drivers accountable for crosswalk accidents, intersection collisions, and more.

Bicycle Accidents

Cyclists deserve safe roads. We represent riders injured by distracted drivers, dooring incidents, unsafe road conditions, and right-of-way violations.

Workplace Injuries

Beyond workers' comp, third-party injury claims can provide full compensation for construction accidents, equipment failures, and toxic exposure.

Medical Malpractice

Surgical errors, misdiagnosis, medication mistakes, and birth injuries. We hold healthcare providers accountable when their negligence causes harm.

Oakland Courts & Local Resources

If you have been arrested or need to appear in court in Oakland, here is what you need to know about the local court system.

Rene C. Davidson Courthouse

1225 Fallon Street, Oakland, CA 94612

Main courthouse for Alameda County Superior Court. Handles felony criminal trials, civil unlimited cases, family law, and probate matters. Located in downtown Oakland near Lake Merritt.

Wiley W. Manuel Courthouse

661 Washington Street, Oakland, CA 94607

Handles criminal arraignments, misdemeanor cases, preliminary hearings, and traffic matters for the Oakland area. Also processes restraining orders and small claims cases.

Santa Rita Jail

5325 Broder Boulevard, Dublin, CA 94568

Alameda County's primary detention facility, located in Dublin. One of the largest jails in Northern California. If a family member has been booked at Santa Rita, call us immediately for bail and defense assistance.

Glenn Dyer Jail

550 6th Street, Oakland, CA 94607

Downtown Oakland detention facility used for initial booking and short-term holding after Oakland arrests. Located near the Wiley W. Manuel Courthouse.

Common Legal Issues in Oakland

Oakland's size, economic diversity, and enforcement environment create a distinct set of legal challenges. Some of the most common matters we handle for Oakland clients include:

  • Gun and weapons charges — Oakland PD and Alameda County law enforcement aggressively pursue firearms cases, including possession by a felon, carrying a concealed weapon, and assault with a firearm
  • Robbery and carjacking — These violent felonies carry severe prison sentences under California law, and Alameda County prosecutors seek maximum penalties in Oakland cases
  • DUI on I-880 and I-580 — CHP and Oakland PD patrol these high-traffic corridors heavily; DUI arrests on Oakland freeways are among the most common in Alameda County
  • Sideshow-related offenses — Oakland has intensified enforcement against illegal sideshows, leading to charges for reckless driving, exhibition of speed, vehicle impoundment, and even felony assault if spectators are injured
  • Port and trucking accidents — The Port of Oakland generates massive commercial truck traffic on local streets and freeways, resulting in catastrophic collisions with passenger vehicles, cyclists, and pedestrians
  • Landlord-tenant disputes — Oakland's Just Cause for Eviction Ordinance and Rent Adjustment Program create complex legal obligations for both landlords and tenants, leading to frequent litigation
  • Construction disputes — Oakland's ongoing development boom generates contractor payment disputes, construction defect claims, and mechanic's lien litigation at a high rate

Morgan Hill's blend of small-town character, wine country, and rapid suburban growth creates a range of legal issues that affect residents and business owners alike. Some of the most common matters we handle for Morgan Hill clients include:

  • Winery and agricultural disputes — The surrounding wine country and agricultural operations produce contract disputes, labor conflicts, land use disagreements, and partnership breakups that require attorneys familiar with both business law and the local industry
  • Small business conflicts — Morgan Hill's expanding commercial districts along Monterey Road and Tennant Avenue generate lease disputes, vendor conflicts, and business-to-business litigation

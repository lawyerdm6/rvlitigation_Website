Common Legal Issues in San Jose

San Jose's size and diversity mean residents face a wide range of legal challenges. Some of the most common matters we handle for San Jose clients include:

  • DUI arrests — San Jose PD and CHP conduct regular DUI checkpoints along Santa Clara Street, Story Road, and major freeway on-ramps
  • Drug charges — Possession and sales cases are frequently prosecuted in Santa Clara County, particularly in East San Jose and downtown
  • Car accidents on Highway 101, 280, and 680 — San Jose's congested freeways see thousands of collisions each year
  • Tech industry employment disputes — Non-compete violations, trade secret theft, and wrongful termination claims are common in Silicon Valley
  • Landlord-tenant disputes — San Jose's rent control ordinance (Apartment Rent Ordinance) generates significant litigation between landlords and tenants
  • Domestic violence charges — SJPD responds to thousands of domestic calls annually; many result in arrests even when the facts are unclear
  • Real estate disputes — With median home prices above $1.3 million, property transaction disputes and boundary conflicts are high-stakes matters

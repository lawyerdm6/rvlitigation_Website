San Mateo County Courts & Facilities

San Mateo County's court system is centralized in the county seat of Redwood City. If you have been arrested or must appear in court, here is what you need to know.

San Mateo County Superior Court

400 County Center, Redwood City, CA 94063

The primary courthouse for all San Mateo County matters. Handles felony and misdemeanor criminal cases, civil unlimited and limited cases, family law, probate, and small claims for the entire county.

South San Francisco Courthouse

South San Francisco, CA

Handles a limited calendar of traffic and minor criminal matters for the northern portion of San Mateo County. Most cases are directed to the main courthouse in Redwood City.

Maguire Correctional Facility

300 Bradford Street, Redwood City, CA 94063

The primary jail and booking facility for San Mateo County. If a family member has been arrested anywhere on the Peninsula, they will likely be booked here. Contact us immediately for bail and defense assistance.

Common Legal Issues in San Mateo County

San Mateo County's position between San Francisco and Silicon Valley creates a distinctive mix of legal issues. Some of the most common matters we handle for Peninsula clients include:

  • Highway 101 and 280 accidents — The Peninsula's two main north-south freeways carry massive commuter traffic daily, producing frequent rear-end collisions, multi-vehicle pileups, and pedestrian accidents at interchanges
  • DUI enforcement — CHP and local police departments conduct regular DUI checkpoints along El Camino Real, Highway 92, and freeway corridors, particularly on weekend nights
  • SFO airport-area incidents — Criminal charges, accidents, and disputes arising near San Francisco International Airport, including rideshare accidents, cargo theft, and commercial vehicle collisions
  • Tech company disputes — With Meta (Facebook) headquartered in Menlo Park and numerous biotech firms in South San Francisco, the Peninsula generates a steady stream of employment disputes, trade secret claims, and business litigation
  • High-value real estate litigation — San Mateo County includes some of the most expensive zip codes in America, and property disputes involving homes in Atherton, Hillsborough, and Woodside routinely involve millions of dollars
  • Coastal development disputes — Half Moon Bay and the San Mateo County coastline are subject to California Coastal Commission regulations, generating conflicts over permits, access, and environmental compliance
  • Commercial real estate litigation — Lease disputes, construction defect claims, and zoning conflicts are common throughout the Peninsula's booming commercial real estate market

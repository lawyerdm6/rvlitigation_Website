CRIMINAL LAW

Criminal Defense

When you're facing criminal charges, your future is on the line. At RV Litigation Group PC, we understand the gravity of your situation and fight relentlessly to protect your rights and freedom.

Our criminal defense practice covers a comprehensive range of cases including DUI/DWI charges, drug offenses, theft and property crimes, assault and violent crimes, white-collar crimes, and federal offenses.

We believe everyone deserves a vigorous defense. Our approach combines meticulous case preparation, strategic negotiation, and aggressive courtroom advocacy when necessary.

PERSONAL INJURY

Personal Injury

When negligence causes harm, victims deserve justice and fair compensation. Our personal injury practice is dedicated to helping individuals and families recover from accidents and injuries caused by others.

We handle cases involving automobile accidents, motorcycle and truck accidents, slip and fall injuries, workplace injuries, medical malpractice, and wrongful death claims.

We work on a contingency fee basis for personal injury cases, meaning you don't pay unless we win. Our commitment is to maximize your recovery while you focus on healing.

CIVIL LAW

Civil Litigation

Complex civil disputes require sophisticated legal strategies and unwavering dedication. Our civil litigation practice represents clients in a wide range of business and personal legal matters.

Our expertise spans contract disputes, business litigation, real estate disputes, employment matters, partnership and shareholder disputes, and insurance claims.

Whether through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or trial, we pursue the most effective path to resolve your dispute and achieve your objectives.

OUR CIVIL PRACTICE

Defense for Businesses and Individuals Facing a Civil Lawsuit

Most civil lawsuits are not covered by insurance. When a business or a private individual is sued over a contract, a partnership dispute, a real estate transaction, or another business matter, there is often no insurance company stepping in to provide a defense — which means retaining your own attorney to protect your finances, your property, and your future. At RV Litigation Group PC, we represent businesses and individuals who are facing a civil claim without an insurer to defend them, bringing focused determination and strategic thinking to every matter we handle throughout California. Please note: we represent civil clients on a retainer basis and do not offer contingency-fee arrangements for individuals seeking to bring a lawsuit.

Our civil litigation attorneys serve clients across the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Jose, Santa Clara County, Gilroy, Morgan Hill, and the greater Silicon Valley region. We represent individuals, startups, established businesses, and property owners in disputes that range from straightforward contract breaches to multi-party commercial conflicts requiring sophisticated trial strategy.

From the initial case evaluation through discovery, motion practice, mediation, and trial, our team works to position your case for the strongest possible resolution. We are committed to transparent communication and a results-oriented approach that keeps your goals at the center of every decision.

PRACTICE AREAS

Civil Litigation Services

Contract Disputes

Breach of contract claims, specific performance, and damages recovery for business and personal agreements.

Business Litigation

Corporate disputes, partnership conflicts, unfair competition, and trade secret protection for California businesses.

Real Estate Disputes

Property disputes, boundary issues, title claims, disclosure violations, and HOA conflicts throughout the Bay Area.

Employment Disputes

Wrongful termination, workplace discrimination, harassment claims, and wage and hour violations.

Landlord-Tenant Disputes

Eviction matters, lease disputes, security deposit claims, and habitability issues under California law.

Construction Defects

Structural defects, water intrusion, code violations, and builder liability under California's Right to Repair Act.

Cannabis / Marijuana Law

Cannabis business licensing, regulatory compliance, Proposition 64 matters, and dispensary zoning disputes.

Zoning & Land Use

Zoning variances, conditional use permits, CEQA challenges, and land use entitlements across California.

Insurance Claims / Bad Faith

Denied and underpaid claims, bad faith litigation, and policyholder rights under the California Insurance Code.

Partnership & Shareholder Disputes

Partnership dissolution, fiduciary duty breaches, minority shareholder oppression, and buyout disputes.

Breach of Fiduciary Duty

Claims against trustees, corporate officers, partners, and agents who violated their duty of loyalty, care, or good faith.

Mechanics Lien Claims

Lien recording, enforcement, and defense for contractors, subcontractors, and property owners under California's mechanics lien statutes.

Unfair Business Practices (UCL)

Claims under California's Unfair Competition Law (Business & Professions Code 17200) targeting unlawful, unfair, or fraudulent business acts.

Trade Secret Litigation

Misappropriation claims under the California Uniform Trade Secrets Act. We protect proprietary information and seek injunctive relief and damages.

Non-Compete Disputes

Enforcement and defense of restrictive covenants, non-solicitation agreements, and employee mobility disputes under California law.

Commercial Lease Disputes

Lease breaches, rent disputes, CAM charges, tenant improvements, and commercial eviction matters for landlords and tenants.

HOA Disputes

Homeowner association assessment disputes, CC&R enforcement, election challenges, and board governance litigation.

Wage & Hour Claims

Unpaid wages, overtime violations, meal and rest break claims, and misclassification disputes under California labor law.

Wrongful Eviction

Illegal lockouts, retaliatory evictions, and violations of local rent control ordinances. We fight to protect tenant rights and recover damages.

Quiet Title Actions

Resolving competing claims to real property ownership, clearing title defects, and establishing clear legal title through court action.

Partition Actions

Court-ordered division or sale of co-owned property when owners cannot agree. We represent co-owners seeking fair resolution of shared real estate.

Breach of Warranty

Express and implied warranty claims for defective products, construction, and services under California commercial and consumer law.
